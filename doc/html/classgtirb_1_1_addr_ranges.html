void gtirb::AddrRanges::addRange ( std::pair< gtirb::EA, gtirb::EA >  x)

Add a pair of EA's to the internal vector. This will perform a check on the EA's to ensure that the first is less than or equal to the second.

Throws a gtirb::RuntimeError if the EA's are not properly ordered.
